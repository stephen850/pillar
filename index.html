About Pillar Ops

THE OPERATIONAL
BACKBONE.

Pillar Ops is operational infrastructure for artists, managers and teams who organise shows worldwide. We handle the professional execution of confirmed shows — without acting as a booking agency or taking commission on bookings.

Our role begins the moment a show is confirmed. From that point, we support artists and their teams with the full operational execution of tours and performances. The live industry is growing globally. Artists operate increasingly internationally and take more control of their own careers — creating a new category: the independent international artist with direct relationships with promoters, but no dedicated operations layer.

Pillar Ops was built to fill exactly that gap.
